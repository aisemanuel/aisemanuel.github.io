Tech Highlights
Bose 700 Headphones

Blocking out the noise.
Anytime you need to zone out during work, on the commute, or while taking a walk - these guys got you covered.

With its active noise cancellation, you can really focus in on what you’re doing an worry less about distractions.

I’m not the biggest audiophile, but the sound quality is great also if you’re considering to use them for online meetings.

iPad Mini 6

My go-to portable powerhouse.
Whether it’s for reading, note-taking, or watching videos, the iPad Mini 6 is the perfect balance of portability and performance.

It’s compact enough to carry anywhere but powerful enough to handle work and creativity on the go. Paired with the Apple Pencil, it’s great for quick sketches, annotating documents, or jotting down ideas.

If you’re looking for a tablet that won’t weigh you down but still packs a punch, this is it.

Keychron K3 Mechanical Keyboard

A keyboard that makes typing enjoyable.
The Keychron K3 is my everyday mechanical keyboard—thin, wireless, and satisfying to type on.

Whether I’m working on projects, replying to emails, or just writing for fun, the tactile feedback makes the experience so much better. Plus, with its compact design, it doesn’t take up too much space on my desk.

If you appreciate a good keyboard but want something sleek and low-profile, this one’s a great choice.
